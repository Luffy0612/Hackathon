About GBPUAT Pantnagar

Govind Ballabh Pant University of Agriculture and Technology, Pantnagar is India's first agricultural university and a pioneer in agricultural education, research, and innovation.

The university has produced thousands of successful alumni working in technology, agriculture, research, civil services, startups, and multinational companies across the world.
